About

I’m Karim Hmaissi an independent front-end web developer living just outside Birmingham UK. I have years of experience building websites, working with clients, backend developers, designers and digital agencies.

Using HTML5, CSS3 and JavaScript I can bring a website to life. Websites I build are responsive, easy to use, performant, beautiful and accessible by you, your customers and search engines.

While I am not a designer I do have a keen eye for design and a strong understanding of UX and UI principles. I pride myself on developing a front end experience that respects and enhances a designer’s original vision.

With a computer science degree and years of programming experience I have a strong understanding of the technical aspects of web development. This includes the backend and concepts such as MVC, REST, and various web frameworks and technologies such as Node, jQuery, AngularJS, and many others.

I am used to working in a busy fast moving agency environment. I am also comfortable working from my home web studio or in your office with your team. Whether it is bringing me in for a brand new project or bringing a project back from a fast approaching deadline I can hit the ground running.
